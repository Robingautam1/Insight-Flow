Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption (AES-256) for all data at rest and in transit. We never sell your data.

Can I connect my own database?

Yes, on the Business plan you can connect directly to PostgreSQL, MySQL, and BigQuery.

Is there a mobile app?

InsightFlow is fully responsive and works perfectly on your phone's browser. A dedicated native app is coming soon.
